Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How does DistriX optimize deadhead trips for truckers?

DistriX leverages an AI-powered system to match truckers with deadhead trips to the original routes of their cargo deliveries. This ensures that truckers have a load to carry on their return journey, maximizing revenue potential while offering transportation at a significantly lower fee due to the deadhead nature of the trip.

2. How can wholesalers upload their products onto the DistriX web app?

Wholesalers can access the DistriX web app using their assigned credentials and navigate to the product upload section. They can fill in the required product details, upload product images, specify shipping requirements, and submit the product listing for verification and approval by the DistriX operations team.

3. Can retailers track the status of their orders?

Yes, retailers can track the status of their orders through the DistriX web app. They can view updates on order processing, pickup, and delivery stages. Additionally, they can communicate directly with the truckers for real-time updates on the delivery status.

4. How can I provide feedback or report an issue with the DistriX operations?

You can provide feedback or report any issues or concerns by contacting the DistriX operations team directly. They will address your feedback and work towards resolving any issues promptly to ensure a smooth experience within the DistriX network.
